Browser security prevents direct links to shortcut settings, so follow these steps to customize your key:

  1. Copy this exact text: chrome://extensions/shortcuts (or edge://extensions/shortcuts if you are on Edge).
  2. Paste it into your browser's top address bar and press Enter.
  3. Scroll down to Gemini Quiz Assistant.
  4. Click the pencil icon and press your preferred key combination!
